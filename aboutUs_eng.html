ABOUT US

¿What is a Cheese-Pap?

It’s a snack based on cheese and homemade French fries, where any topping can be added to them. This snack is great for sharing with Friends during parties, or with family. What we aim to achieve is to get a nostalgic feeling from the 80’s with each bite, since during that time this was the most common snack.

Our Mission

We are a snack that, besides being delicious, we bring families and friends together on their happiest times, creating a whole experience with us.

Our Vision

We see ourselves 5 years into the future in a place near the center of the city, in a big establishment where families and friends can spend their time together and have fun at the same time. We also see ourselves having 2 or more franchises, which would be smaller than the original, but with the same essence that characterizes us.

Our Values

  • Empathy: we put ourselves in our clients’ shoes in order to treat them how they deserve to be treated, giving them the best service, and also applying this treatment to our coworkers, since empathy is essential to have great relationships.

  • Effort: We put our biggest effort and dedication to grant that our clients are satisfied and happy. Our service and quality of our products is our priority.

  • Gratefulness: In our enterprise we dedicate ourselves to show and transmit to our clients our gratitude for having chosen our product to enjoy.

  • Responsibility: We are conscious and we assume our obligations as an enterprise and as suppliers to offer products of the best quality possible, with the best of treatments and service for our clients.
