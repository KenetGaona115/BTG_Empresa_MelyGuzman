Mission, Vision and Values

Our Mission

Colored Crepe is a company that is different from others because our product is simple and common, but we decided to innovate by making it from different colors, so our creativity and imagination becomes clear.

Our Vision

Acknowledge our team that sells different color crepes, satisfy our clients palate, and make a difference.

Our purpose

Show to the public something completely new and different from a common crepe.

Our values

  • Commitment: This value is necessary because our customers deserve quality products to meet your needs with good service.

  • Responsibility: Organize each of the procedures to provide good care to patients. customers, as well as obtaining quality products for the preparation of the final product that we offer in the market.

  • Efficiency: We deliver quality results in a timely manner at the time our client want.

  • Awareness: We are aware of and assume our commitments as a company and as sellers to offer the products of the highest possible quality with the best treatment and service to our customers.
